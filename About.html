Avery Chang (they/he) b. 1995: My focus is New Media Arts and Sculpture. My art exists in conversation with glitch artists and LGBT/Global Asian Activists as well as social justice movements for prison abolition, sex worker rights, and anti-fascist campaigns. As a Chinese-Filipino transgender person, I explore topics about diverse queer experiences. I enjoy reimaging queer futures utilizing abstract, sci-fi and fantastical elements. My works include trans bodies, creatures and abstractions, as well as reclaimed Asian mythology. I explore queer entities in otherworldly environments invoking curiosity and playfulness towards cryptids, creatures and othered bodies. One of my goals is to explore and reclaim the fear associated with otheredness.  I seek to do this with experiments with tactical materials environments, interactive video games and robotics, and experimental fabrication techniques.
